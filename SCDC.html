Great Place to Learn

At Sree Chaitanya Degree College, we equip students with 28 spacious classrooms, computer labs with 60 computers with internet facility like no other college in the surrounding areas of Kavali, prodigious seminar hall with capacity of holding 150 students at a time, a library contained with 5000 volumes and well equipped physics, chemistry and biotechnology laboratories. Needless to say that we make it a great place to learn.

Behind Our Success...

Our success is the amalgamation of parallel efforts put forth by our management to mold students into industry standard outcomes and our students who made this possible by believing in us and coordinating their support since the day one.
